About This Trek

Ali Bedni Bugyal is one of the finest high-altitude meadow treks in Uttarakhand — and one of the lesser-known ones, which is part of its appeal. The route climbs steeply from Lohajung through dense forests to reach Ali Bugyal, then traverses across rolling meadows to Bedni Bugyal — one of the most expansive and beautiful bugyals in the Garhwal Himalaya.

The views from Bedni Bugyal are exceptional: Trishul (7,120m), Nanda Ghunti (6,309m), and on clear days, the Nanda Devi massif. In May–June, the meadows bloom with wildflowers; in September–October, the grass turns golden and the skies are crystal clear.

This is also the approach route to Roopkund — a longer expedition for another season. As a standalone trek, Ali Bedni offers everything a bugyals trek should: forest trails, dramatic meadow camps, and peaks that fill the horizon.

Who Is This For

  • Trekkers with moderate fitness looking for their first proper alpine meadow experience
  • Those wanting a less-crowded alternative to Dayara or Brahmatal
  • Groups wanting panoramic Kumaon Himalayan views without extreme altitude
  • Families with fit teenagers (15+)

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Kathgodam to Lohajung 2,350m ▾
Drive from Kathgodam (nearest railhead) through Almora and Kausani to Lohajung — a small village that serves as the base for multiple Roopkund-area treks. ~8 hrs drive. Briefing and gear check in the evening.
Day 2 Lohajung to Didna Village 2,750m ▾
Trek begins with a steep climb through forest and past terraced fields to Didna — a traditional Garhwali village. The views of the surrounding ridges open as you gain height. Walk: ~5 hrs.
Day 3 Didna to Ali Bugyal 3,350m ▾
Climb through forest that gradually opens into the lower reaches of Ali Bugyal — vast, rolling alpine grasslands stretching across the ridgeline. First views of Trishul and Nanda Ghunti. Camp in the meadow. Walk: ~6 hrs.
Day 4 Ali Bugyal to Bedni Bugyal 3,700m ▾
Traverse across the ridge from Ali to Bedni — a landscape of open meadows, glacial lakes, and panoramic Himalayan views. Bedni Bugyal is wider and more dramatic than Ali, with the Trishul-Nanda Ghunti-Nanda Devi arc filling the horizon. Walk: ~4 hrs.
Day 5 Bedni Bugyal – Exploration 3,700m ▾
A free day at Bedni — explore the upper meadows, walk to the Bedni Kund (glacial lake), watch the light change on Trishul. One of the most peaceful campsites in the Uttarakhand Himalaya.
Day 6 Bedni Bugyal to Lohajung 2,350m ▾
Full descent back to Lohajung through meadows and forest. The same route in reverse, but different light, different mood. Walk: ~7 hrs.
Day 7 Lohajung to Kathgodam ▾
Drive back to Kathgodam. Trek ends. ~8 hrs drive.
