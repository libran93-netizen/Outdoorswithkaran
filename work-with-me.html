What I Offer

Three ways to work together

01

Himalayan Treks & Expeditions

Custom Himalayan treks and peak expeditions for private groups — from a first Himalayan weekend to a 6,000m summit attempt. Led personally by Karan, safety-first from day one.

  • Private group departures — your dates, your pace
  • Trek selection, itinerary planning & logistics
  • Full safety & medical management (WFR certified)
  • Equipment guidance & pre-trek preparation

School & Youth Outdoor Programs

End-to-end outdoor education programs for schools and youth organisations — Himalayan treks, experiential learning camps, and multi-day edu-tours across India. Designed around learning outcomes, not just activity.

  • Ages 10–18, groups from 15 to 100+
  • Custom itinerary with learning objectives built in
  • Facilitated activities, debrief sessions & reflection

Corporate & Institutional Programmes

Outdoor team-building and adventure programmes for companies, institutes, and large groups. Designed to challenge, connect, and build genuine team cohesion — not just box-tick activities.
